PAPER SUBMISSION

Submission and Review Process of Full Papers and Extended Abstracts

Each submission will be blind reviewed by 2-3 reviewers in the relevant subject domain. Once we have finalized the status of your paper (accepted/accepted with modifications/rejection) it will be informed to you. Since the review of all submitted papers are professional paid work irrespective of the final decision of the submission, the detailed review comments will be provided to authors only if they register for the conference as a participant. Hence, all accepted authors must register for the conference before they submit the final version of their papers. The status of each paper will be informed to relevant authors individually. If they register for the conference, the detailed review will be emailed to the corresponding author.

Types of acceptance:

After the review process, papers are selected for the following three categories. Authors have to edit their papers to satisfy their following requirements while addressing reviewersâ€™ and editorial committee suggestions. Link for the final submission will be published when the review process is over. Final Submission dead line for all the accepted Papers and Extended Abstracts will be on or before 20-Oct-2014.

  • Full paper: Full Papers should be 4-6 Pages, describing the results of completed or cutting-edge technology deployment. Follow the relevant instructions and format your paper according to the guidelines given in the template file on MS Word. Authors will receive 15 minutes presentation and 5 minutes Q&A time period during the conference.
  • Extended Abstract: Should describe the results of completed/ongoing research or cutting-edge technology deployment on a single page summary according to guidelines given in the template files on MS Word. Presentation time will depend on the quality of the work. And printed poster 2 feet width and 3 feet height to be display during the conference.

Please hand over your power point presentations and posters during the 1st week of December.

Workshop or Tutorials

You could propose a workshop or tutorial for the conference which will be a pre or post conference activity on the 10th or 13th December 2014. Please submit Title, One page abstract and CVs of resource persons who will conduct the workshop or Tutorials.

Paper Submission

Submissions Closed.
